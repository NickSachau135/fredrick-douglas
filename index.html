Fredrick Douglass

Fredrick Douglass was an american abolitionist. He was an escaped slave who became a activist, author, and a public speaker. He became the leader in the abolitionist movement, which was to end slavery, before and durring the civil war. He was very dedicated to achieving justice for all americans, mainly African Americans, Woman, and minority groups.
